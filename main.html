UI, UX & IA

IA

focuses on organizing, structuring, and labeling content in an effective and sustainable way; site maps, hierarchies, categorizations, navigation, metadata, etc.

UX

focuses on having a deep understanding of users, what they need, what they value, their abilities, and also their limitations; user personas, user journeys, flowchart, analytics, site maps, sketches, etc.

UI

focuses on anticipating what users might need to do and ensuring that the interface has elements that are easy to access, understand, and use to facilitate those actions; mood board, wireframes, mockups, assets, style guide, code
